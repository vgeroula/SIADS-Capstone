Data access statement

As it is also mentioned in the "README" file, the data used for this project is accessible and can be downloaded from the following link:
https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/competitive-data-science-predict-future-sales/data

It mainly consists of daily historical sales data. Our task is to be able to forecast the total amount of products sold in every shop.

Based on the above kaggle URL, "this dataset is permitted to be used for any purpose, including commercial use".
